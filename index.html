Support & FAQ

How do I play?

Swipe in any direction to control your snake. Eat food to grow longer and score points. Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail!

Can I customize my snake?

Yes! Tap the customize button on the main menu to choose different colors and patterns for your snake.

The game is running slowly. What should I do?

Try closing other apps running in the background. VipeRun is optimized for the latest iOS devices. Make sure your device is updated to the latest iOS version.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

I found a bug. How can I report it?

Please contact us using the email below with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version. We appreciate your feedback!

What are the system requirements?

VipeRun requires iOS 15.0 or later and is compatible with iPhone and iPad.
